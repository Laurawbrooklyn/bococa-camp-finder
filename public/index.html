Find the perfect camp for your kids

BococaCampFinder helps you find the perfect summer camp for your child in the Bococa Neighborhood of Brooklyn and surrounding areas including: Boreum Hill, Cobble Hill, Carroll Gardens, Columbia Street Waterfront and Brooklyn Heights (including camps that offer bus pickups in those neighborhoods as well). Search by a specific area, age range, type of camp and cost per week. And you can login to add camps too!

About BCF

BococaCampFinder was created to make the job of finding a summer camp for your child much easier!

BococaCampFinder was created by local mom, Laura Wallace, after talking with fellow mom friends and realizing the lack of a central place to find out about summer camp options within their Brooklyn neighborhood.
